LUCIA GARAVITO


ABOUT ME

Hello everybody, my name is Lucia and I come from Bogotá, Colombia. I am currently studying at Algonquin College in Ottawa city. I arrived to Canada on December 11th on a pretty windy night, I am not going to lie, the trip was a total odyssey, but I arrived safe and sound. So far, it has been a great experience. I love the snow and how everything turns white. However, What I do not like about winter is the cold and the days are way shorter than back home, it turns dark around 5 pm (Crazy, I know).

I enjoy travelling and meeting new people from different cultures, I lived in Argentina for several years and it became my second home, I hope Canada earns a spot in my heart as well. In my spare time, I like to read, draw, and watch movies and tv shows.(One of my favorites is GOT) Aside from this, one of my biggest passions is to learn languages, I speak Spanish, English, and French, and I was learning Korean and Italian but did not finish; hopefully, I will retake them in the near future. I enjoy dancing and listening to any genre of music.

So this is a small scope of who I am and do not forget:
Whatever you do, always give 100%. Unless you’re donating blood.” —Bill Murray
